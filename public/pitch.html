The problem

$2.4 trillion in skills and goods sits idle — because the only tool for trading it is cash people don't have.

A hairstylist needs a logo. A developer needs childcare. A baker needs legal help. Each has exactly what another wants — but no money changes hands, so the deal never happens. Existing barter is a 1:1 coincidence-of-wants problem that almost never resolves. We solve it with AI.

The solution

AI finds the swap — or the chain.

Claude-powered matching scans the whole network for direct trades and multi-party loops: You → them → someone else → back to you. Everyone gets what they want. No cash required.
